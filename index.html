Questions

FAQ

Is Casino Alpha legal in my country?

That depends on your local law and the operator's licences. The brand holds a placeholder licence, but a foreign licence is not the same as authorization in your jurisdiction. Check your national regulator's register before registering.

How long do withdrawals take?

In my test, within the stated 0–48 hour window after verification. First withdrawals take longer because of KYC. Method and country also matter.

Do I have to take the welcome bonus?

No. Bonuses are optional and come with wagering requirements. If you prefer flexible withdrawals, you can decline the bonus at deposit time.

What documents are needed for verification?

Typically a government ID and proof of address; sometimes proof of payment method. Exact requirements are listed in the operator's verification policy.
