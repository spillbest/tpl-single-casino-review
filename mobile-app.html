Questions

App FAQ

Is there a native iOS or Android app?

Not at the time of my test. Mobile access works through the browser or the installable web app. Check the operator's site for current availability in your country.

Does the web app take up storage like a normal app?

Only a small amount. A PWA is essentially the website cached locally, so it occupies far less space than a store app and updates itself.

Are all games available on mobile?

Nearly all modern titles are built mobile-first. A handful of older games may be desktop-only; the lobby hides what your device cannot run.

Is playing on mobile data safe?

The connection is encrypted either way. Avoid public Wi-Fi for cashier operations, keep your OS updated, and use a screen lock since the web app keeps you signed in.
