First-session checklist

What to check after logging in

Five minutes in the account settings can save you trouble later. These are the items I verify on every platform I review.

  • Verification status — upload KYC documents before, not after, you want to withdraw
  • Deposit, loss and session limits — set them while your judgement is fresh
  • Two-factor authentication, if offered
  • Registered email and phone number are correct
  • Bonus opt-in status — make sure you have not joined an offer unintentionally
  • Transaction history is visible and exportable

Questions

Login FAQ

I forgot my password — what now?

Use the password-reset link on the login form. The reset email arrived within a minute in my test. If it does not arrive, check spam and then contact support via live chat.

Why is my account blocked after several attempts?

Repeated failed logins trigger a temporary lock as an anti-fraud measure. Wait the stated period or contact support with your account email to unlock it.

Can I have more than one account?

No. One account per person is a near-universal rule; duplicates risk confiscated balances. If you lost access to an old account, recover it instead of registering again.

Why am I asked for documents at login?

Operators must verify identity and age under their licence obligations. A documents prompt usually means KYC is incomplete — finishing it early prevents withdrawal delays.
