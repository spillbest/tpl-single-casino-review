Plain language

What the bonus means

A typical welcome package combines a deposit match and free spins. None of it is free money: every part carries wagering requirements and limits.

Deposit match

The casino credits a percentage of your deposit as bonus funds. Bonus funds are locked until wagering is complete — you cannot simply withdraw them.

Wagering requirement

A multiplier (for example x35) applied to the bonus or bonus-plus-deposit. Until you stake that total, winnings from bonus play stay restricted.

Caps and exclusions

Maximum bet while wagering, maximum win from free spins, excluded games and expiry dates. These are where most players get caught out.

Verification checklist

How to check the offer and its terms

Before opting in, open the bonus terms page on the operator's site and confirm each point below. If a term is unclear, ask live chat and save the answer.

  • Exact wagering multiplier and what it applies to
  • Game weighting — slots usually 100%, tables far less
  • Maximum bet allowed while the bonus is active
  • Expiry window for the bonus and the free spins
  • Whether your payment method qualifies for the offer
  • Country availability — offers differ by jurisdiction

Questions

Bonus FAQ

Can I withdraw the bonus immediately?

No. Bonus funds are locked until the wagering requirement is met. Only fully wagered winnings become withdrawable, subject to the bonus terms.

Can I decline the bonus?

Yes, and in some cases it is the sensible choice. Declining keeps your deposit unrestricted. Look for an opt-out option in the cashier or ask support before depositing.

Do all games count towards wagering?

Usually not equally. Slots often count 100%, while table and live games count partially or not at all. The weighting table is part of the bonus terms.

Is the bonus the same in every country?

No. Amounts, spin counts and even availability differ by jurisdiction. The offer you see after registering with your real address is the one that applies.
